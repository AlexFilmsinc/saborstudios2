Privacy Policy

How Sabor Studios handles inquiry details, project communication, and website information.

Last updated July 17, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a project inquiry, Sabor Studios may collect your name, email address, phone number, project type, preferred date, budget range, and any project details you choose to include.

The website may also use basic technical information, such as browser type, device type, and page interaction data, to understand how visitors use the site and improve the experience.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through the site is used to respond to inquiries, discuss potential projects, prepare estimates, schedule calls or shoots, and manage client communication.

Sabor Studios does not sell personal information.

Project Materials

If you send references, briefs, photos, videos, logos, music, or other materials, those files are used only to evaluate, plan, produce, or deliver the requested project unless another use is agreed to in writing.

Third-Party Services

Inquiry forms, email, social media platforms, analytics tools, video embeds, or scheduling tools may process information according to their own privacy practices. Sabor Studios is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

Data Retention

Inquiry and project information may be kept as long as needed for communication, project records, business operations, tax records, or legal requirements.

Your Choices

You may request that Sabor Studios update or delete your inquiry information by contacting the studio directly. Some records may need to be retained where required for business, accounting, or legal reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Sabor Studios through the Start Project form or official social channels.
